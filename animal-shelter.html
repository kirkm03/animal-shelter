Imaginationland Animal Shelter

Thank you for visiting our shelter! Even in a land of whimsy and dreams, animals sometimes lose their way and have nowhere to go. Our shelter has proudly provided these creatures with a temporary home until they can find their forever home. Maybe that will be with you!

Please take the time to read about several of our featured adoptable pets. If you're interested in adopting one, stop by our shelter, located at the rift between the Good and Evil realms of Imaginationland, to meet them in person.

Squirrely the Squirrel

Small in stature, with a big round head, and short little arms, Squirrely has light, taupe-colored fur, with spots of pale green on his belly, under his tail, and right above his dark red eyes. Around his neck, Squirrely wears a bright red scarf. He speaks in a soft, welcoming voice that is incredibly misleading. He also bears a strong southern accent.

Beary the Bear

Large, pudgy, and round, Beary oughtweighs the other woodland critters. He has thick, dark brown fur, and light brown spots on his snout, and below his brows. He has big, uneven teeth, and stubby claws. He wears a striped red and green winter hat with a single puff ball at the end.

Beavery the Beaver

Beavery has brown fur, and spots of tan on his stomach, and around his eyes. He has big front teeth, and a flat black tail, and toes. He wears a blue winter hat, and gloves.

Chickadee-y the Chickadee

A tiny little bird with a short blue beak, and scrawny legs. Chickadee-y has black feathers, streaks of white on his cheeks, and a round yellow belly. He wears a red and green tie, and is the smallest of the Woodland Critters.

Please note that these animals are very fond of each other, and would prefer to be adopted together if possible. Something about blood orgies...anyways aren't they so cute?!
